Real betting is gambling and illegal in most states. When you make a Parlay bet, you are not making a real bet. A real bet is an agreement in which something of value is at risk. When you sign up for Parlay, you agree that you will not make agreements through the app. Since in a Parlay bet there is no agreement that anyone will give up anything of value, there is nothing of value at risk.

When you send a new Parlay bet to someone, you are saying, "Maybe I will give you X if Y happens." When the receiver taps the green button, they are saying, "Maybe I will give you X if Y doesn't happen." This interaction is not an agreement.
