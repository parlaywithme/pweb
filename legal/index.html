Betting is illegal in most states. When you make a "bet" in Parlay, you are not making a bet by the legal definition of the word. Legally, a bet is an agreement in which something of value is at risk. When you sign up for Parlay, you agree that you will not make agreements in the app and that all money transfers are optional.
